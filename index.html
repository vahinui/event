5TH ANNUAL EVENT
CYCLE THE CONTINENTAL DIVIDE
AUGUST 15st, 2018

The 5th annual Cycle The Continental Divide Trail Race presented by The Mountain Spoke is set to go off on August 15st, 2018. Comprised of some of the most exciting sections of remote trail in the Gallatin National Forest, the Cycle The Continental Divide Trail Race is a challenging course that takes riders over ridgelines, past alpine lakes and down flowing drainages while exploring 40+ miles of remote connected trails.
